# Patterns: Work

## Purpose

Recurring patterns at work: how I take on tasks, how I handle deadlines, what I avoid, what types of work energize me versus drain me, and the psychological residue of work events (1:1s, feedback, deliverables). This overlaps with `patterns/adhd.md` (initiation, transition), `patterns/anxiety.md` (anticipation), and `patterns/self-esteem.md` (worth tied to output) — link generously rather than duplicating.

## Update rules

- A pattern is added only after **at least 3 distinct observations on different days**.
- Distinguish patterns about:
  - **Task shape** (what kind of work goes well or badly)
  - **Time/structure** (when in the day or week things break down)
  - **Social/work boundary** (1:1s, feedback, meetings, async vs. sync)
  - **Self-evaluation tied to output** — note but cross-link to `patterns/self-esteem.md` rather than duplicating
- Note **what conditions reliably produce good work**, with evidence — not just what produces bad work.
- Work patterns interact strongly with sleep, exercise, and medication. Note those as moderators.

## Entry template

```markdown
### <Pattern name>

- **Domain:** task shape / time-structure / social boundary / output-evaluation / other
- **Trigger or context:** <what reliably surrounds the pattern>
- **What happens:** <concrete behavior — observable>
- **Effect:** <impact on output, mood, downstream events>
- **Moderators:** <sleep, structure, accountability, medication>
- **What has helped (with evidence):** <strategies and where they were tested>
- **What has not (with evidence):** <tried and failed>
- **Evidence:** `sessions/...`, `journal/...`
- **Counter-evidence:** <contexts where the pattern would have been expected but did not occur>
- **Confidence:** low / medium / high
- **Last reviewed:** YYYY-MM-DD
```

## Example

```markdown
### Pre-deadline initiation cliff

- **Domain:** task shape × time-structure
- **Trigger or context:** Multi-week deliverables with no externally enforced milestones. First two weeks: drift. Final week: marathon.
- **What happens:** Concrete plan exists but is not executed. Several false starts. Real work begins only when deadline is < 5 days away.
- **Effect:** Work gets done, quality usually acceptable, but cost is high: sleep loss, anxiety spike, weekend loss, post-deliverable crash.
- **Moderators:** Worse on longer timelines. Better when even one externally-scheduled check-in exists in the middle.
- **What has helped:** Self-imposed check-ins do not work. A scheduled show-and-tell with one peer (2026-04) did — once.
- **What has not:** Time-blocking the calendar in advance. Task-list apps. Pre-commitment to a peer who I don't trust will follow up.
- **Evidence:** `sessions/2026-03-15-deliverable-cliff.md`, `sessions/2026-04-28-deliverable-cliff.md`, `sessions/2026-05-26-deliverable-cliff.md`
- **Counter-evidence:** 2026-05-12 deliverable did not cliff — had a co-author who was visibly ahead of me. Social pressure was the active ingredient.
- **Confidence:** medium-high
- **Last reviewed:** 2026-06-09
```
