# Patterns: Work

## Purpose

Recurring patterns at work: how I take on tasks, how I handle deadlines, what I avoid, what types of work energize me versus drain me, and the psychological residue of work events (1:1s, feedback, deliverables). Patterns are **analytical artifacts** supporting `psychological-map.md` (Work Model). This file overlaps with `patterns/adhd.md` (initiation, transition), `patterns/anxiety.md` (anticipation), and `patterns/self-esteem.md` (worth tied to output) — link generously rather than duplicating.

## Update rules

- A pattern is added only after **at least 3 distinct observations on different days**.
- Every entry has the five required fields: **description, evidence, counter-evidence, confidence, experiments.**
- Distinguish patterns about:
  - **task shape** (what kind of work goes well or badly)
  - **time/structure** (when in the day or week things break down)
  - **social/work boundary** (1:1s, feedback, meetings, async vs. sync)
  - **self-evaluation tied to output** — note but cross-link to `patterns/self-esteem.md` rather than duplicating
- Note what conditions reliably produce **good work**, with evidence — not just what produces bad work.
- Work patterns interact strongly with sleep, exercise, and medication. Note those as moderators.
- Patterns may *propose* updates to `psychological-map.md` but never replace it.

## Entry template

```markdown
### <Pattern name>

- **Description:** <plain-language description: domain (task shape / time-structure / social boundary / output-evaluation), trigger or context, what happens, effect>
- **Evidence:** `sessions/...`, `journal/...`
- **Counter-evidence:** <contexts where the pattern would have been expected but did not occur>
- **Confidence:** low / medium / high
- **Experiments:** `experiments/...` (or "proposed: <one-line idea>")
- **Moderators:** <sleep, structure, accountability, medication>
- **What has helped:** <strategies and where they were tested>
- **What has not:** <tried and failed>
- **Map status:** not promoted / proposed for map / present in map (`section`)
- **Last reviewed:** YYYY-MM-DD
```

## Example

```markdown
### Pre-deadline initiation cliff

- **Description:** Task-shape × time-structure pattern. Multi-week deliverables with no externally enforced milestones drift for the first two-thirds and become a marathon in the final week. Concrete plan exists but is not executed until deadline < 5 days. Work usually gets done at acceptable quality, but with sleep loss, anxiety spike, weekend loss, and post-deliverable crash.
- **Evidence:** `sessions/2026-03-15-deliverable-cliff.md`, `sessions/2026-04-28-deliverable-cliff.md`, `sessions/2026-05-26-deliverable-cliff.md`
- **Counter-evidence:** 2026-05-12 deliverable did not cliff — had a co-author visibly ahead of me. Social pressure was the active ingredient.
- **Confidence:** medium-high
- **Experiments:** proposed: weekly Friday show-and-tell with one peer for the next multi-week deliverable.
- **Moderators:** Worse on longer timelines. Better when one externally-scheduled mid-point check-in exists.
- **What has helped:** Scheduled mid-point check-in with a peer.
- **What has not:** Self-imposed check-ins. Time-blocking the calendar. Task-list apps.
- **Map status:** proposed for map (Work Model)
- **Last reviewed:** 2026-06-09
```
