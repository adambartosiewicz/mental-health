# Patterns: Self-esteem

## Purpose

Recurring patterns in how I evaluate myself: the conditions under which self-worth wobbles, the inner narratives that recur, the events that lift or sink it, and the relationship between self-evaluation and behavior. Patterns are **analytical artifacts** supporting `psychological-map.md` (Self-esteem Model).

This file is **especially prone to drift into rumination** if treated as a place to log every bad day — it is not. It is a place to map the *shapes* of self-evaluation that repeat.

## Update rules

- A pattern is added only after **at least 3 distinct observations on different days**.
- Every entry has the five required fields: **description, evidence, counter-evidence, confidence, experiments.**
- Distinguish:
  - **trigger-linked dips** — self-esteem drops reliably after specific events
  - **baseline patterns** — how self-evaluation looks when nothing in particular has happened
  - **compensatory behaviors** — what I do *because of* a self-esteem dip (over-working, over-apologizing, withdrawing, performing)
- **No instance-logging.** A single day of low self-esteem belongs in `journal/`, not here. Patterns are recurrences.
- **No interpretation as fact.** "I have low self-esteem because of X in childhood" is a hypothesis, not a pattern. Note it as an open question in `psychological-map.md` if it recurs.
- Watch for **self-criticism as a pattern in itself** — the loop, not just the content.
- Patterns may *propose* updates to `psychological-map.md` but never replace it.

## Entry template

```markdown
### <Pattern name>

- **Description:** <plain-language description: type (trigger-linked / baseline / compensatory), trigger if any, inner content (paraphrased), behavior it produces, time course>
- **Evidence:** `sessions/...`, `journal/...`
- **Counter-evidence:** <times the trigger occurred but the dip did not — what was different>
- **Confidence:** low / medium / high
- **Experiments:** `experiments/...` (or "proposed: <one-line idea>")
- **Linked open questions:** <section in `psychological-map.md`>
- **Map status:** not promoted / proposed for map / present in map (`section`)
- **Last reviewed:** YYYY-MM-DD
```

## Example

```markdown
### Worth-tied-to-output

- **Description:** Trigger-linked. Days with no visible deliverable produced — regardless of whether real work was done — produce a dip. Inner content: "didn't earn the day," comparison to a vague more-productive version of self. Behavior: reluctance to rest in the evening, sometimes overwork the next morning to "make up," sometimes withdraw from partner conversation. Resolves overnight if the next day has visible output. Lingers across multiple low-output days.
- **Evidence:** `sessions/2026-04-30-no-output-day.md`, `sessions/2026-05-21-no-output-day.md`, `journal/2026-06-03.md`
- **Counter-evidence:** Did not occur on 2026-05-08 — low-output day but spent time with friends. Social engagement may substitute for output.
- **Confidence:** medium
- **Experiments:** proposed: deliberately schedule one no-output day per week, log whether the dip appears.
- **Linked open questions:** `psychological-map.md` → Open Questions (worth-vs-output)
- **Map status:** proposed for map (Self-esteem Model)
- **Last reviewed:** 2026-06-09
```
